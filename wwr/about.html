Our Mission

Hello from Austina! We are committed to offering the best thrilling and safe whitewater rafting adventures on earth. Our skilled staff ensures that each journey is a memorable excursion for you and your friends.

History

Founded in 2005, Austina started with one boat and a love for the water. Since then, we have grown to many more boats and a group of thirty professional staff. Our goal has always been to show the wonder of the nature through the rush of the waves.
