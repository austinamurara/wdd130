Zimbabwe

Zimbabwe is a stunning country in Southern Africa known for its rich history and breathtaking natural wonders. It is home to the mighty Victoria Falls, one of the world's largest and most spectacular waterfalls. The country also boasts the Great Zimbabwe ruins, an ancient stone city that serves as a testament to a powerful historical civilization. Beyond these landmarks, Zimbabwe is famous for its diverse wildlife and warm hospitality.
